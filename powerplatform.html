Personal Productivity PowerApp

To keep myself organized and I decided to create ansimple productivity tracker app using Microsoft PowerApps, a rapid development software that I gained experience with during my internship this past Summer. The app tracks my schedule and all my assignments. I can easily create new events or tasks from within the app. The app also tracks how many days in a row I complete all my tasks, I find this is motivating for me to keep my streak going for as long as possible.
